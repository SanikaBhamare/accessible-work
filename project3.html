Accessible Work Environment

Lead Researcher: Nikhil Verma

In Progress

This research project aims to make ChatGPT accessible for blind individuals worldwide. By developing an enhanced version of ChatGPT, the project seeks to understand how blind users interact with large language models (LLMs). This version will serve as a research tool to study blind users' experiences, focusing on accessibility features and prompt engineering to improve usability for the visually impaired. The findings will provide valuable insights into designing more inclusive AI-driven tools.
